This page is intended for you, who wish to understand the procedures that SASI is adopting to comply with the General Data Protection Law.

SASI's activities

SASI promoted advances in systems, internal policies, processes, as well as constantly adapting to the LGPD. In this way, SASI has been promoting training for its employees and periodic reviews of its documents. SASI holds documents to ensure that our relationship with you, our users, as well as with our customers/developers, is supported, namely: Terms of Use (user and distributor/customer) and Privacy Policy.

Terms of Use - Distributor/Customer and User

Privacy Policy

In addition to ensuring safe treatment for the user, the platform offers fields that can be customized by the developer/client so that he can enter the Terms of Use for his SAPps (SASI Apps). In this way, the developer prepares a document according to the specifics of the Application created for its users, according to their respective purposes. Finally, we offer a channel in the SASI application itself for users to effectively exercise their rights, that is, after the user logs in, he will be able to access a page, which provides fields for his requests regarding data.

Furthermore, the request can also be made via email [email protected]

Requests

To access your information and request the adjustment or withdrawal of any registration data with SASI or its inactivation, simply send an e-mail to [email protected] or access the Application and click on the field REQUESTS FROM HOLDER to carry out your request. SASI will promptly respond to your rights and take the necessary steps so that your matter is evaluated within the specified period. The definitive deletion of the data can be made upon request, as well as will be carried out by SASI after the end of its purpose of use.

Data collect

The personal data provided by you or collected by SASI to allow you to subscribe to and use SASI services will depend on the type of plan on the SASI platform, and may include: user name, email, telephone, date of birth, gender, geographic location , residence and country. SASI collects data from its customers through the Login registration in the application.
